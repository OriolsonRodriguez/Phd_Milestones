\vspace{1.5em}

\paragraph{Principal Supervisor — Prof.\ Michael Bode}
Prof.\ Bode is an applied mathematician and conservation scientist whose work focuses on
decision-making under uncertainty, network ecology, and mathematical biology.
His expertise in optimisation and agent-based modelling directly informs the computational
backbone of this project.

\paragraph{Associate Supervisor — Prof.\ Jonathan Rhodes}
Prof.\ Rhodes specialises in spatial ecology, conservation planning, and the modelling of
coupled human–natural systems.
His experience bridging quantitative models with real-world policy provides critical
grounding for the treaty-system applications explored in this research.
